Charles Babbage: The First Puter


Life time/Youth


Charles Babbage was born on December 26, 1791 in Walworth, Surrey. Babbage lived until he was 79 and died on October 18, 1871

Babbage attended Trinity Cambridge, in 1810 to study mathematics. Babbage graduated without honors from Peterhouse in 1814 and received a master of arts in 1817.

Babbage would later marry Georgiana Whitmore and had eight whole children with her. But sadly only three out of those eight would live to be adults.

Babbage would later be a mathematic professor at Campbridge University. He was a professor from 1828 to 1839.
